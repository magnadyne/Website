The Story

Kingdom of Lexington, 1333 AD

Local peasant, Amrita Magnadyne, finds herself on death row for thievery, sentenced to execution by guillotine. One by one, the prisoners are slaughtered until Rita is placed in the guillotine. However, just as the blade descends, a blinding light erupts from the cathedral's stained glass.

Moments later, Rita awakens to find herself alive and outside the guillotine. A hellscape lies around her. All of the townsfolk mutilated. With the main gate locked behind her and many questions to be answered, she ventures inside Castle Lexington.





The Castle

Explore every hallway, tunnel, and crevice

A large castle awaits you, containing demons, secret passages, and powerful foes. Find new abilities, weapons, armor, and supplies as you journey through. A treasure trove of unique items awaits you...

Eternal Soul contains more than 12 unique areas and over 100 different enemies. There are also several characters that you will find along your journey, some friends and some foes.

The Angel

The soul of a angel rests within you

You have been blessed with the angel «Amaranth Death», thus granting you with two crucial abilities:

  • IMMORTALITY : No longer are you bound by mortal life. If you fall in combat you will be revived at your previous resting point. All progress and items are kept, but other characters will remember your follies.

  • SOUL STEAL : Kill demons for a chance to obtain their soul and harness their power. Or steal the souls of demons within others, and use their abilities against them.




The Demons

A litany of beasts roam the castle

Every demon you defeat contains a unique soul that you have a chance of obtaining. The soul can be one of the four following types:

  • MANIFEST souls create a projectile or attack. Such as fire balls, knives, bullets, etc.
  • AUGMENT souls provide some type of alteration to the player. Such as a stat boost, transformation, or manipulation.
  • GUARDIAN souls summon the demon to fight on your behalf. They can be set as completely autonomous (SENTIENT) or only acting when commanded (COMMAND).
  • PASSIVE souls provide a constant effect at no energy cost. These range from stat boosts to permanent abilities.

You can obtain an unlimited number of souls and equip one of each type - with the exception of passive souls, which have no restriction.

Certain souls can be leveled up. With a max level of 1-9. A soul's level increases based on its quantity.

Additional Info


  • What kind of game is this?
    • Eternal Soul is a Metroidvania game: an open ended RPG, with 2D platformer controls, and item / ability-based progression.

  • When will the full game release?
    • There is currently no set release date. One will be established as we near the end of development.

  • What platforms will it be released for?
    • Windows & Linux, which includes native Steam Deck support.
    • Xbox, Playstation, and Switch versions will all be worked on after the PC release.
    • Android, iPhone, and Mac OS will be considered if there is enough demand.

  • Can I beta test?
    • Sure, if you join the newsletter! Our beta testers will be chosen from there.

  • Can I join the development team?
    • We're always looking for talented pixel artists and composers (music/SFX) to join.
    • If interested, send examples of your work to contact@barrierstudio.com.
